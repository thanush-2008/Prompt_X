AI Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

1. Overview

PROMPT_X is built around the use of artificial intelligence tools for photo editing. This AI Policy explains how AI is used on our platform, what you can expect from our prompts and generator, and your responsibilities when using AI-related features.

2. Nature of Our Prompts

The prompts in our library are written instructions designed to be used with AI image-editing tools (such as Gemini, ChatGPT, or similar models). PROMPT_X does not create, train, or own the underlying AI models referenced. We simply curate and provide prompt text.

3. In-Browser Generator / Editor

Our Generate / Edit Image tool allows you to preview how a prompt might be applied to your photo. Depending on availability, actual image generation may be powered by third-party AI services. Generated results are provided "as is" and may not always be available, accurate, or of consistent quality.

4. No Guarantee of Output

AI-generated results can be unpredictable. We do not guarantee that any prompt will produce a specific result, and outputs may vary between attempts, models, and updates to underlying AI systems.

5. Responsible Use

You agree not to use PROMPT_X prompts or generated images to:

  • Create misleading, deceptive, or defamatory content about real people
  • Generate non-consensual or inappropriate imagery of any individual
  • Violate any third party's intellectual property or privacy rights
  • Produce content that violates applicable laws or platform policies of AI providers

6. Uploaded Images

When you upload an image to our generator, it is processed for the purpose of previewing your edit. You are responsible for ensuring you have the right to use and edit any image you upload, including images containing other people, with their consent.

7. AI Content Disclosure

Images produced via our generator are AI-generated or AI-assisted. If you use such images publicly, we encourage you to disclose that they were created or modified using AI, in line with platform guidelines and applicable regulations.

8. Continuous Updates

AI technology evolves rapidly. Prompts, features, and underlying models may be updated, replaced, or removed at any time without prior notice as we improve the Service.
